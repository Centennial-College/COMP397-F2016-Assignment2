Background Story

You are the lone soldier who defends his country from alien invaders known as tetrominoes!
You must use your particle launcher shoot to kill them before they reach your base. If they reach your base, your country will get destroyed. You must kill a certain number of enemies to advance to the next level. You will emerge victorious as your country's hero when you slay all thy enemies...

Controls

Press on the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to navigate your particle launcher across your country's borders.

Press on the SPACE key to fire your particle launcher. You must wait for it to recharge in between shots. Press 1, 2, 3, or 4 to switch between bullets as you unlock them through levelling up.
